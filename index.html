Relevant Links

Links to things that might be useful while you're here at Syracuse.

  • Health services

    Visit the Health Services website for information about booking an appointment, to order free sexual health supplies through their Safe Sex Express, for a list of what's going around on campus, and many other health-related functions.
    Call the office at 315-443-9005.

    Regular office hours:
    Monday 8:30AM - 7:00PM
    Tuesday 8:30AM - 7:00PM
    Wednesday 8:30AM - 5:00PM
    Thursday 8:30AM - 5:00PM
    Friday 8:30AM - 5:00PM
    Saturday 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
    Sunday Closed

  • Orange After Dark

    Looking for something to do on a Friday night? Orange After Dark's got you covered. A list of cheap, fun events can be found on their website.

  • Career Services

    Career Services is a great resource for Major and Career counseling, resume and letter feedback, assistance with finding internships and jobs, advice regarding networking and interviews, and many other super helpful functions. Drop by their office in 235 Schine for 15 minute drop-ins Monday and Tuesday 12:00-2:30pm | Wednesday and Thursday 2:00-4:30pm.

  • Department of Public Safety

    For information about public safety, vsit the Department of Public Safety (DPS) website. For assistance, call 315-443-2224.
